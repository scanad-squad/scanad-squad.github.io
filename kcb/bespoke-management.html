Wealth Management Services Bespoke Management

Bespoke Wealth Solutions is a product for High Net Worth (HNW) and Ultra High Net Worth (UHNW) customers.

KCB IB proposition is to structure and tailor-make custom portfolios for each customer based on their uniqueness of Investment objectives, risk tolerance, time horizon, liquidity need and any other special circumstances. This product explores opportunities across different asset classes, counterparties, jurisdiction, onshore and offshore.

Wealth Management Services
