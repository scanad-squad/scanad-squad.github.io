Brokerage Services CDSC Services

KCB Investment Bank facilitates all CDSC processes as listed below:

  • CDS Account Opening
  • Settlement of shares bought and sold
  • Immobilization of shares held in certificate form
  • Transfer of a CDS account to KCB Investment Bank
  • Pledge of shares in favour of banks and financial institutions
  • Release of pledged shares
  • Private transfer of shares
  • Transmission of shares in case of death or succession
